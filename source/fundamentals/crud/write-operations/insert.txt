=================
Insert a Document
=================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

Overview
--------

In this guide, you can learn how to insert documents into your MongoDB
database. To insert a document, use insert operations.

Insert operations consist of the ``insertOne()`` and ``insertMany()``
methods. For both methods, the driver automatically generates a unique
``_id`` for documents unless specified. 

Insert Operations
-----------------

insertOne()
~~~~~~~~~~~

Use the ``insertOne()`` method to insert a single document. This method
returns an ``InsertOneResult`` which tells us the ``insertedId`` of the
document inserted.
